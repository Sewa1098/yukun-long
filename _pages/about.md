I am **Yukun Long**, a Computer Science student at the **University of New South Wales (UNSW)** and currently an exchange student at **The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)**.

My work focuses on the intersection of **technical development, game design, and interactive media**. I am particularly interested in how computational systems can be combined with creative design to build meaningful digital experiences.

My academic and personal projects span across multiple areas, including:

- game design and level development  
- interactive storytelling  
- XR and immersive experiences  
- web-based systems and software development  
- creative technology experiments  

Beyond technical implementation, I also enjoy exploring how digital projects communicate ideas visually and emotionally. This has led me to work on projects that combine programming, interface design, narrative structure, and media production.

Currently, I am building my portfolio through coursework, independent projects, and experimental prototypes that reflect both technical capability and creative direction.

This site serves as a collection of selected works, projects, and ongoing explorations.

I enjoy building projects that sit between logic and imagination.
